📖 Our Story

Exam Saarthi was born from a simple idea - every student deserves free access to quality study materials. What started as a small initiative by two passionate students has now become a trusted platform for thousands of learners across India.

Gourav Sharma

Developer & Founder
Indira Gandhi University, Meerpur, Rewari
A passionate developer and final year student at IGU Rewari, Gourav envisioned a platform where students could easily access previous year question papers without any hassle. His technical expertise and dedication brought Exam Saarthi to life. He believes in "Code for a Cause" and aims to make education accessible to all.

Mahesh Verma

Content Manager & Founder
Indira Gandhi University, Meerpur, Rewari
With a deep understanding of student needs, Mahesh curates and verifies all content on the platform. His commitment to quality ensures that every PYQ is accurate and helpful for exam preparation. His mantra is "Quality content changes lives" and he ensures every resource meets the highest standards.

Indira Gandhi University, Meerpur, Rewari

Both founders are proud students of Indira Gandhi University, Rewari, Haryana. This prestigious university has been the foundation of their journey, and through Exam Saarthi, they aim to give back to the student community that has supported them throughout their academic life.

Our Mission

To empower every student with free, easy, and reliable access to previous year question papers and exam resources. We believe that no student should struggle to find quality study materials during their crucial exam preparation phase.

Our Vision

To become India's most trusted platform for academic resources, helping millions of students achieve their educational goals through accessible, organized, and verified study materials - all completely free of cost.

Why We Do It

As students ourselves, we know the struggles of exam preparation. The endless search for previous year papers, the cost of buying guides, and the frustration of finding unreliable resources - we've been there. Exam Saarthi is our way of making exam preparation simpler, more organized, and completely free for everyone.
